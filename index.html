Our Journey

We come from two different cultures (Medanese and Javanese) each with its own unique stories, flavors, and traditions.
But fate had a beautiful plan.

Brought together by shared values and guided by faith, we began our relationship with sincerity and hope.
From the very start, he was drawn to her grace and quiet strength, her beauty matched only by her calm presence.

One of the joys that brought us even closer was our love for culinary adventures. Every dish, every café, every bite became part of our story.

We’ve walked side by side through life’s highs and lows, especially during one of his most difficult chapters. Through unwavering support and shared dreams, we’ve grown stronger together.

Now, as we prepare to begin this new chapter as husband and wife, we pray that love continues to be our foundation, patience our strength, and faith our guide, through every step of this journey ahead.
